Bio

Regan Willis is a programmer and artist interested in using computers to create human connections and improve our relationships with our digital selves.

She is a Software Engineer at ASSET, LLC. where she works on healtcare technology by augmenting internal systems, building web apps, and using machine learning to find patterns in data.

Regan graduates from the University of South Carolina in 2021 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems, a minor in Business Information Systems, a minor in Data Science, and an Information Assurance Specialization.
