Hi, I'm Regan. I'm a programmer with a focus in applied machine learning and a passion for writing readable code and amplifying underrepresented voices in computer science. I graduated from the University of South Carolina in 2021 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems, a minor in Business Information Management and an Information Assurance Specialization.

I work for Integer Technologies, a federal contractor startup in Columbia, South Carolina, where I build perception system software for uncrewed surface vessels (USVs). I serve as the lead engineer in our company partnership with a SC HBCU consortium that aims to increase the number of Black computer science students earning engineering jobs after graduation.
